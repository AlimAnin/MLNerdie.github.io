Machine learning has touched every aspect of our life also as India is heading towards Digital India, the IT industry will require 50% more workforce equipped with digital skills. Professionals from data engineering and ML space are in high demand and companies are grappling to fill these positions. Finding and attracting ML and data science talent has become a strategic imperative for every company.

Thus with computer science and ML applications becoming more pervasive in everyday life, people from a non-technical background should also join the field.

People with non-tech background may find it difficult to start a career in ML but its not a hard job at all, they could easily excel in ML as all one need is to brush up some mathematical concepts like linear algebra, statistics, calculus etc and ML Nerdie is always there to guide you!

MLNerdie is an independent newsletter to make machine learning more accessible to everyone and since everything today is related to ML so everyone should get a chance to learn ML irrespective whether they have a technical background or not and this is the main objective of MLnerdie. We conduct webinars ,teaching ML from the scratch and taking beginners step by step towards machine learning.

MLnerdie provides students a platform to showcase their work and ask questions that are easier to ask among peers than in class and lectures.

MLnerdie serves as a platform to promote diversity in tech and especially guiding and helping people with non technical background to start their career in ML.
