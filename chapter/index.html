MLNerdie Los Angeles

Director: Abi Aryan

Abi is a machine learning researcher and currently working as a research scholar with Dr. Judea Pearl studying autonomous agents. Her research interests are learning systems, multi-agent systems, ML OSS and procedural content generation. Previously, she worked as a consultant in machine learning and data science with various startups for four years and teaching machine learning sessions. Abi is also the Director at Women Who Code Los Angeles and a reviewer and area chair for WiML at NIPS 2018.

Contact: la@mlnerdie.com

MLNerdie New Delhi

Director: Shreya Gupta

I am a dissident of the conventional approaches to machine learning and aim to understand the world behind the three lines of code (import, train and test). I have worked on foundational mathematics and NLP to understand language and its connection to human perception and cognition. I am passionate about creating equal opportunities in tech for all genders and races. Always up for good conversations, art and volleyball.
